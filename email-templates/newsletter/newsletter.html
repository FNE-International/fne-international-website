Featured Story

Building Futures: How Education is Transforming Communities in Peru

In the highlands of Peru, access to quality education has long been a challenge for rural communities. This year, FNE International partnered with local schools to provide educational resources, teacher training, and after-school programs that are making a real difference.

"Before, many children would walk hours to reach the nearest school, often arriving too tired to learn. Now, students have a safe place to study."

— Rosa, Local Teacher

The new learning center

The new learning center provides a safe, welcoming environment where children can focus on their studies. With proper desks, lighting, and educational materials, students are thriving like never before.

Thanks to your generous support, over 150 children now have access to educational materials, tutoring, and nutritious meals.

Health & Wellness

Medical Outreach Reaches 200+ Families

Our recent medical mission brought essential healthcare services to underserved communities in the Dominican Republic. Over three days, a team of volunteer doctors, nurses, and health workers provided free checkups, vaccinations, and health education to more than 200 families.

"We are so grateful. My children finally received the care they needed. For many of us, this was the first time we had seen a doctor in years."

Ana, Mother of Three
